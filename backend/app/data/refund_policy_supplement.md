# Shopward Refund Policy — Product Category Supplement

Version 3.3 · Effective Date: January 15, 2026

## Purpose

This supplement extends the Shopward Refund and Returns Policy with category-specific refund requirements. Where this supplement conflicts with the primary policy, the primary policy Decision Priority Rules apply.

## Product Classification

Products are classified as: Physical Consumer Goods, Electronics, Computer Hardware, Mobile Devices, Clothing & Apparel, Footwear, Home & Kitchen, Furniture, Books, Digital Downloads, Software Licenses, Gift Cards, Subscription Services, Beauty & Cosmetics, Personal Hygiene Products, Medical Devices, Toys & Games, Luxury Products, Collectibles, and Grocery & Perishable Goods.

## Digital Downloads and Software Licenses

Digital downloads and software licenses are non-refundable once access has been granted or the license key has been delivered. Customers may request review only for duplicate billing or technical failure preventing activation within 14 days.

Automatic refund decision: **Denied** for standard change-of-mind returns.

## Gift Cards

Gift cards are non-refundable once issued or activated. Gift cards cannot be redeemed for cash and cannot be returned for a refund to the original payment method. Exceptions require manual review for duplicate purchase or technical delivery failure.

Automatic refund decision: **Denied** unless duplicate billing is confirmed.

## Subscription Services

Subscription products require cancellation through subscription support. Prorated refunds are not automatically issued for partial billing periods. Change-of-mind cancellation after activation requires manual subscription review.

Automatic refund decision: **Escalated** to subscription support.

## Grocery and Perishable Goods

Grocery items, fresh food, beverages, and other perishable goods are eligible for refund only when:

- The item arrived spoiled, damaged, or past the stated freshness date.
- The customer reports the issue within 48 hours of delivery.
- Photographic evidence of the product and packaging is provided.

Change-of-mind returns on perishable goods are **not** eligible. Opened consumables without a quality defect are **denied**.

## Beauty and Cosmetics

Unopened beauty products may be returned within the standard window. Opened cosmetics, skincare, or hygiene-sensitive beauty items cannot be refunded automatically due to health and safety regulations.

## Personal Hygiene Products

Hygiene-sensitive products (masks, serums, personal care items) must be unopened and sealed for automatic refund approval. Opened hygiene products are denied unless a manufacturing defect is documented with evidence.

## Electronics

Electronics require serial number verification, matching product identification, and functional inspection. Opened electronics may incur a 15% restocking fee unless the return is due to manufacturer defect, wrong item delivered, or shipping damage.

## Clothing and Apparel

Returns accepted when original tags remain attached, product is unwashed, and product has not been altered. Sizing issues are generally eligible within the standard return period.

## Footwear

Shoes must not have been worn outdoors, should include the original box where available, and must not show excessive sole wear.

## Product Image Verification

When customers upload product images, verification compares product category, brand, model, colour, visible serial number, packaging, and condition. Up to three upload attempts are allowed for mismatch or low-quality images before escalation to manual review.

If the uploaded image does not match the purchased product (for example, a different item category or model), the customer must upload a new image. Low-resolution or blurry images must be retried before manual review.

## Refund Evaluation Outcomes

- **Approved**: Product category, condition, return window, and customer reason align with policy.
- **Denied**: Category restrictions apply (digital, gift card, perishable change-of-mind, opened hygiene, final sale).
- **Escalated**: Evidence mismatch after retries, ambiguous reason, subscription, or manual verification required.
