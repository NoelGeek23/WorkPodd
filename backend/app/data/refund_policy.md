# Shopward Refund And Returns Policy

Version 3.2
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

## Purpose

This Refund and Returns Policy defines the conditions under which customers may request returns, refunds, exchanges, replacements, store credits, or order cancellations for products purchased through Shopward.

All refund decisions must comply with this policy. Customer support representatives, automated systems, and AI agents are not permitted to grant exceptions unless explicitly authorized by sections within this policy.

## Definitions

Order Date: The date on which an order is successfully placed.

Shipment Date: The date on which the order leaves the warehouse.

Delivery Date: The date confirmed by the shipping carrier as delivered.

Return Window: The period during which a return request may be submitted.

Refund: A monetary reimbursement issued to the original payment method.

Store Credit: A non-cash credit issued to the customer's account.

Exchange: Replacement of a purchased item with another item.

Final Sale Item: A product designated as non-returnable and non-refundable.

VIP Customer: A customer with lifetime purchases exceeding $5,000.

## General Return Eligibility

A customer may be eligible for a return if all of the following conditions are met:

- The return request is submitted within the applicable return window.
- The item was purchased directly from Shopward.
- Proof of purchase is available.
- The product is not listed as non-returnable.
- The item has not been intentionally damaged.
- The customer is not under refund abuse investigation.

Failure to satisfy any condition may result in automatic denial.

## Standard Return Window

- Physical products: return requests must be submitted within 30 calendar days of delivery.
- VIP customers: return requests may be submitted within 45 calendar days of delivery.
- Business accounts: return requests must be submitted within 15 calendar days of delivery.
- Requests submitted after the applicable window will be automatically denied.

## Product Categories Not Eligible For Refund

The following products are non-refundable:

- Digital downloads.
- Gift cards.
- Subscription fees.
- Downloadable software licenses.
- Personalized products.
- Customized products.
- Engraved products.
- Opened hygiene products.
- Opened cosmetics.
- Opened medical devices.
- Clearance products marked final sale.

No exceptions may be granted by automated systems. Manager approval is required for any exception request.

## Condition Requirements

Returned items must meet all of the following:

- Original packaging included.
- Original accessories included.
- Original manuals included.
- Original serial numbers intact.
- Product not modified.

Products returned with missing components may receive partial refund, store credit, or rejection depending on assessment.

## Damaged On Arrival Products

Products damaged during shipment qualify for special handling.

Requirements:

- Customer must submit claim within 7 days of delivery.
- Photographic evidence is required.
- Packaging images are required.

Approved remedies:

- Full refund.
- Replacement.
- Store credit.

Customer may choose preferred resolution.

## Wrong Item Delivered

If Shopward ships an incorrect item:

Requirements:

- Claim within 14 days of delivery.
- Photographic evidence required.

Resolution:

- Full refund.
- Replacement shipment.
- Return shipping costs are paid by Shopward.

## Lost Shipment Policy

Orders marked as lost by the carrier qualify for investigation.

Conditions:

- 5 business days after expected delivery date.
- Carrier investigation completed.

Resolution options:

- Full refund.
- Replacement order.

Manager approval is not required.

## Return Shipping Responsibility

Customer is responsible for return shipping unless:

- Product arrived damaged.
- Wrong item delivered.
- Product has manufacturer defect.
- Shopward shipping error occurred.

Otherwise, customer pays return shipping costs.

## Manufacturer Defects

Products exhibiting manufacturing defects qualify for return within 90 days of delivery.

Evidence required:

- Photos.
- Videos when applicable.

Possible outcomes:

- Repair.
- Replacement.
- Refund.

Decision determined by product category.

## Open Box Electronics

Electronics returned after opening are subject to a 15% restocking fee.

Exceptions:

- Manufacturer defect.
- Wrong item delivered.
- Damaged during shipment.

Restocking fee is waived in those cases.

## Subscription Products

Subscription services are generally non-refundable.

Exceptions:

- Duplicate billing.
- Technical failure preventing service activation.

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days.

## Fraud Prevention Rules

Refund requests may be denied when:

- Identity verification fails.
- Payment fraud indicators exist.
- Multiple accounts share suspicious information.
- Excessive refund behavior is detected.

Automatic review thresholds:

- More than 3 approved refunds in 12 months.
- More than $2,000 refunded in 12 months.
- More than 5 return requests in 90 days.

These requests require manual review.

## Refund Abuse Policy

Customers may be flagged for abuse if they:

- Repeatedly return heavily used items.
- Frequently claim damage without evidence.
- Manipulate return windows.
- Attempt duplicate refunds.

Flagged accounts may lose refund privileges for a minimum duration of 12 months.

## VIP Customer Exceptions

VIP customers may qualify for exceptions.

Permitted exceptions:

- Return window extension to 45 days.
- Waived restocking fees up to $250.
- Expedited replacement processing.

VIP status does not override:

- Final sale restrictions.
- Gift card restrictions.
- Digital product restrictions.

## High Value Orders

- Orders exceeding $500 require additional review.
- Orders exceeding $1,500 require manager approval before refund issuance.
- Orders exceeding $5,000 require senior manager approval.
- Automated systems may recommend but cannot approve such refunds.

## Bulk Orders

Orders containing more than 20 units of a product are considered bulk purchases.

Bulk purchases:

- Are not eligible for standard returns.
- Are subject to business account rules.
- Require manager approval.

## International Orders

International orders:

- Must be returned within 20 days of delivery.
- Customer pays return shipping.
- Customs fees and import taxes are non-refundable.

## Refund Processing Times

Approved refunds:

- Credit Card: 5-10 business days.
- Debit Card: 5-15 business days.
- Bank Transfer: 7-20 business days.
- Store Credit: immediate.

Processing times begin after approval.

## Exchange Policy

Exchanges are permitted when:

- Product is defective.
- Incorrect size received.
- Wrong item shipped.

Exchanges are not guaranteed when inventory is unavailable. Store credit may be issued instead.

## Store Credit Rules

Store credits:

- Never expire.
- Cannot be redeemed for cash.
- Cannot be transferred.

Store credit may be issued instead of refunds at Shopward discretion.

## Escalation Matrix

- Level 1: AI Support Agent.
- Level 2: Customer Service Representative.
- Level 3: Refund Manager.
- Level 4: Senior Operations Manager.

Mandatory escalation:

- Orders over $1,500.
- Fraud investigations.
- VIP exception requests.
- Legal disputes.

## Decision Priority Rules

When policy conflicts occur, apply the following order:

1. Fraud Prevention Rules.
2. Final Sale Restrictions.
3. Product Category Restrictions.
4. Return Window Rules.
5. VIP Exceptions.
6. Customer Satisfaction Guidelines.

Higher priority rules override lower priority rules.

## Examples

Example 1: Customer purchased headphones, delivered 20 days ago, item unopened. Decision: approved. Reason: within return window.

Example 2: Customer purchased digital software and requested refund after 2 days. Decision: denied. Reason: digital products are non-refundable.

Example 3: VIP customer requests return after 40 days. Decision: approved. Reason: VIP exception allows up to 45 days.

Example 4: Customer has received 5 refunds in past year. Decision: manual review. Reason: exceeds fraud review threshold.

Example 5: Customer requests refund for final sale item. Decision: denied. Reason: final sale restriction overrides return window eligibility.
